## Overview

BeigeBox is available under a dual-license model:

- **AGPL v3 (Free)** — For open-source and non-commercial use
- **Commercial License (Paid)** — For proprietary and commercial use

## When You Need a Commercial License

You need a commercial license if you:

- Build a proprietary/closed-source product that **incorporates or bundles BeigeBox**
- Run BeigeBox as a **managed service** and charge customers for it
- Modify BeigeBox and want to **keep modifications private** (AGPL requires sharing)
- Want to use BeigeBox in a **commercial environment** without open-source obligations

## When You DON'T Need a Commercial License

You can use BeigeBox under AGPL v3 if you:

- Use BeigeBox as-is (no modifications) for internal purposes
- Build an **open-source project** using BeigeBox
- Modify BeigeBox but **publicly release your modifications** under AGPL v3
- Run BeigeBox internally for your own business (no customer-facing service)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I modify BeigeBox under AGPL and keep it private?**
A: No. AGPL requires that if you modify BeigeBox and offer it as a service, you must share modifications. For proprietary use, get a commercial license.

**Q: If I use BeigeBox internally, do I need a commercial license?**
A: No, if it's internal-only. AGPL's network clause applies only to distributed modifications. Internal use is fine.

**Q: Can I take BeigeBox, integrate it into my SaaS, and use AGPL?**
A: Only if you open-source your entire SaaS. If you want it proprietary, you need a commercial license.

**Q: What if I contribute my modifications back under AGPL?**
A: Great! You comply with AGPL and don't need a commercial license. We'll merge good PRs and credit you.

**Q: Can I switch from AGPL to commercial later?**
A: Yes. Start with AGPL, and if your use becomes commercial, contact us to transition to a commercial license.
